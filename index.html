Bio

Biography I’ve always been driven by the need to create, to build something from the ground up, and to turn ideas into reality. My journey as an entrepreneur started when I was just 14. My friends and I ran a lawn care business, and while it might not sound like much, it taught me the value of hard work, persistence, and earning trust. Those lessons stayed with me as I grew older and began exploring bigger opportunities.

As an adult, I found a niche in buying and selling appliances from apartment complexes—taking what others overlooked and turning it into a thriving business. I eventually sold that company to Guaranteed Appliance in San Marcos, which gave me both the confidence and capital to move into a new chapter of my life.

From there, I launched a construction company focused on renovating apartment complexes. It wasn’t easy, but I poured everything I had into building a business that delivered results and built strong relationships. Over time, the company earned a reputation for quality and reliability, backed by a proven track record and solid references. Eventually, I sold that business too, feeling ready to tackle a completely different frontier.

That’s when I pivoted to the world of software and crypto. I’ve always been fascinated by innovation, and this space gave me the chance to apply my problem-solving skills in entirely new ways. Over the years, I launched several startups, each one teaching me valuable lessons about technology, scaling, and staying agile. One of my proudest achievements during this time was creating a produce delivery app, which I sold to Mom and Pop 2 Go.

Now, after years of iterating on businesses and successfully exiting three of them, I’ve come full circle. I’m back to building in the physical world—real structures, with real impact. This time, I’m bringing everything I’ve learned along the way: the discipline from running my first lawn business, the resilience from scaling startups, and the precision from managing construction projects.

This next phase of my life isn’t just about building—it’s about applying a lifetime of experience to create something lasting and meaningful. Every step of the journey has shaped me, and I’m excited to see what’s next.
